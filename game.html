Card Matching Game

The objective of the game is to find and match all pairs of cards. The base score is 20.

  1. First click on a card to reveal the image then click on another card to find the matching pair.
  2. After revealing two cards and if they do not match, the score will be deducted by 1 and the cards will be hidden again. If the cards match, you have found a pair.
  3. The game ends when all pairs are found. For each score that you've earned after completing the game, you are awarded a $0.10 discount per score if you are logged in.
